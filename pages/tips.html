Tips

These tips for travelling will have you saving money, sleeping better, getting off the beaten path more, meeting locals and just being a better traveler.

  • Pack Light.
  • Use a small backpack/suitcase.
  • Always pack a towel.
  • Take an extra bank card and credit card with you.
  • Always visit the local tourism information center.
